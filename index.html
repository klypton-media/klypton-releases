about

KLYPTON is an advanced video analysis & clip creation tool for Windows.

It downloads YouTube videos and helps you create optimized clips — manually via our editor, automatically via keywords or ✨AI analysis, or algorithmically via scene analysis...

... then outputs your .mp4 clips, combined summary video, and text transcripts.

Video & Audio
  • List videos in a YouTube playlist or channel
  • Sort videos by likes, follower count, length, date, etc
  • Batch convert YouTube URL links to short form
  • Extract video, audio, and transcript from YT videos
  • Extract audio to MP3 and 7 other audio formats
  • Create SRT subtitle files and 6 other formats
  • Burn-in (hard coded) subtitles to video
  • Find keywords in transcript / get kw density report
  • Fix/enhance video (speed, rotation, brightness...)
  • Fix/enhance audio (normalize audio, remove noise...)
  • Add text or watermark to video
AI & Analysis
  • ✨ AI-keyword clip detection with context awareness
  • ✨ AI-automatic clip detection (no keywords needed)
  • ✨ AI-rewrite video titles with SEO optimization
  • ✨ AI-generate summaries
  • ✨ AI-fix subtitle capitalization & punctuation
  • Analyze clips to reverse-engineer viral settings
  • Create and save custom viral detection profiles
  • Subtitle keyword matching (exact/related/contains)
  • Detect scene cuts and transitions
  • Audio and motion detection
  • Process up to 100 videos in a single batch

✨ AI-powered features require OpenAI api key
